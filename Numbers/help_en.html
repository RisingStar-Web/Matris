Matris

Rules

Matris is a mathematical game similar to Tetris. The 6х9 playing field is being filled with cubes with digits from 1 to 9. Your task is to liberate the field from the cubes that disappear if the sum of the adjacent cubes is equal to or multiple of 10.

The game has 7 modes:

Line - the cubes disappear if the sum of any quantity of the cubes is divisible by 10 horizontally.

2 digits - the cubes disappear if the sum of 2 cubes is equal to 10 horizontally or vertically

3 digits - the cubes disappear if the sum of 3 cubes is multiple of 10 vertically or horizontally.

4 digits - the cubes disappear if the sum of 4 cubes is multiple of 10 vertically or horizontally.

Square 2x2 - the cubes disappear if their amount in any 2x2 square is multiple of 10.

Rectangle 2х3 - the cubes disappear if their amount in any 2x3 rectangle is multiple of 10.

Square 3x3 - the cubes disappear if their amount in any 3x3 square is multiple of 10.

As in Tetris the falling speed of the cubes is constantly increasing.
